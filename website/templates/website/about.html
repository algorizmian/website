About Us

We're a small full stack software consulting company based in London. We have expert knowledge of Python development, and we're also highly proficient in frontend development and DevOps engineering. Over the years, we've helped clients in a variety of industries, including retail, finance, insurance, and IoT to successfully deliver their IT projects.

If you need help with anything involving Python, microservices, APIs, refactoring, clean code, elusive bugs, or code review, feel free to reach out to us!
